Sharing a Fargo based blog post with Radio3

Tweeting direct from a Fargo post is so two to three weeks ago now so I decided to have a crack at creating a little bit of script to run within the Fargo editor to add a "Share article with Radio3" link at the bottom of any articles I write.

How do I do this?

  • Open your menubar.opml within Fargo

  • Copy and paste the following into a free line

    • Add Share article with Radio3

    • var t = op.getLineText ();

    • var et = encodeURIComponent(t);

    • var link = op.getCursorUrl();

    • r3 = "http://radio3.io/?title=" + et + "&link=" + link + "&description=" + et;

    • op.insert ("Share article with Radio3", right);

    • op.attributes.setOne ("type", "link");

    • op.attributes.setOne ("url", r3);

    • op.attributes.setOne ("icon", "rss");
