De La Soul - Smell the DaISY - (Da Inner Soul Yancy)

This album is not only FREE it's also brilliant - I've listened to it countless times and the reworking on certain tracks in the style of Dilla are outstanding. Having been a fan of De La Soul since the release of 3 feet high and rising in 1989. I listened to that tape so much it was uber stretched and sounded pretty nasty after a while!

It's great that these fellas are still able to smash. Really enjoyed their show at Glastonbury as well. Shame about the strong language!

This review just about sums it up

There's also a DJ I've been following for a good few years called United States of Audio (on Twitter :fa-twitter:) and a little while back he did this mix for Solid Steel. Some clever stuff going on in this mix and the tunes are sweet. Go get it!
